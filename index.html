EXPLANATION

How to read this document

Please note that your Smart Contract Protection Policy only starts from page 3 onwards. To help you read and understand your policy better, we are providing some explanations and examples. These are not part of your policy and should not be used to interpret your insurance coverage.

Words in bold

You will notice that some words in the policy are printed in bold letters. This means that they have been given specific meaning in your Smart Contract Protection Policy. Please refer to Section XXIII for the definitions and meanings of these words. 

What makes up your insurance policy? 

A quick note that an insurance policy is a contract between you and us. The terms set forth lay out the intentions of the policy, the coverages, exclusions, terms, and conditions. This contract is made up of:

  • The insurance policy on pages 3-20;
  • The information you provided when you applied for coverage;
  • Any additional Schedules;
  • Any Endorsements attached to the policy; and
  • Your Digital Insurance Proof of Coverage.

All of these, together, make up your insurance contract. It is important to read them all in context of each other. Of course, we are here to help if you get confused. 

When is your coverage effective?

This insurance is effective from the time you purchase coverage or at the agreed time of commencement. Your coverage effective date will be recorded and reflected on your digital proof of insurance.

How much coverage do I need? 

To be safe, we recommend that you insure the total amount of your stake across all available protocols. In the event of a covered loss, our research shows that it is rare that just a portion of assets are impacted.

Do’s and Dont’s After a Loss

Do:
  • Check claims.asknimblr.com for any potential hacks or vulnerabilities regarding your coverage;
  • Inform us as soon as possible about any incident which may give rise to a claim;
  • Report any occurrence to the appropriate law enforcement officials as applicable;
  • Practice security best practices to protect any other digital assets in your possession, custody, or control;
  • Provide us with any additional information regarding the claim.
Do Not:
  • Negotiate or discuss the claim and impact of the claim with any unauthorized third parties;
  • Authorize asset recovery without first discussing with us.

  • COVERED CAUSES OF LOSS



  • Smart Contract Vulnerability

 

We cover loss from protocol smart contracts only as follows:

 

  1. Code being used in an unintended way that results in loss of user digital assets;
  2. Unauthorized, malicious, or criminal acts exploiting a vulnerability in the protocol smart contract;
  3. Design failures resulting in confiscation or seizure of digital assets deposited into the protocol.


  • Oracle Failure Events

 

We cover loss from oracle failure, only where the oracle has been:

 

  1. Deliberately manipulated so that it is materially outside observed market rates, terms and conditions; or
  2. Data is materially different from the intended data source; or
  3. Failure results in either the unintended liquidation of collateral or unintended extraction of funds from the designated protocol.

We cover loss of your digital assets only where the protocol loses your funds by: 

  1. Moving funds to another address which is not the original owner’s address, beneficiary address, or another authorized address; or
  2. Erroneous transfer of funds resulting in permanent and irrecoverable loss of funds;

  • Loss Determination

This section explains how We will settle Your claim once a claim is accepted and payable under Section 1. If you suffer digital asset loss as a result of any incident, we have the option of doing the following:

  • If You Have Lost The Total Amount Covered on This Policy

If, at the time of loss, a loss assessment indicates that the total amount of funds lost equals or exceeds the amount covered on this policy, we will:

 

  1. Pay the lesser of the total coverage amount on this policy as indicated and recorded on your Nimblr Coverage NFT, or the total amount of the loss; and
  2. Terminate your current coverage, policy, and limits without penalty to you. A new policy may be started to represent a new stake or digital asset coverage.


  • If You Have Lost a Portion of the Amount Covered on This Policy

If, at the time of loss, a loss assessment indicates that the total amount of funds lost is less than the amount covered on this policy, we will:

 

  1. Pay the amount of the loss, not to exceed the total amount covered on this policy and indicated and recorded on your Nimblr Coverage NFT; and
  2. Record this claim on your current Nimblr Coverage NFT and Nimblr ID; and
  3. Coverage Amount of your policy will be reduced by the amount paid under this loss.


  • Claim Payment Calculation

At the time of loss, your claim payment will be calculated by:

 

  1. Total impacted amount as determined by blockchain transaction analysis; or
  2. A Loss Assessment prepared through a Nimblr Oracle; or
  3. A Loss Assessment and impact details provided by the protocol; or
  4. Any combination of items (a) through (c) above.


  • Coverage Parameters

Claim validation and payout determination is performed automatically through our authorized oracle system. The process works as follows:

 

  1. Event Detection: The oracle monitors for covered events affecting protocols and protocol users.
  2. Impact Verification: The oracle validates which wallets were impacted by the coverage event, and then:
    1. Transaction data is analyzed to determine the exact amount of loss per wallet;
    2. Verification occurs on-chain to ensure transparency and accuracy.
  3. Payout Execution: Once the oracle confirms the event and impact, payout are automatically calculated, and:
    1. Each eligible wallet receives compensation based on their verified losses;
    2. Payments are executed directly to the verified wallet address;
    3. All transactions are recorded on-chain for verification.

 

The oracle determination is final and authoritative for all coverage decisions related to this policy. Coverage amounts are capped at the maximum coverage limit specified in your Nimblr Coverage NFT.
